About Ctrl-f

We decided to make Ctrl-f as part of the Google Hackathon. We believe that everyone should have access to digestible and concise information. Ctrl-f is meant to help students better understand their study or research material and even assist with citing papers and documents — minimizing plagiarism and making it easier than ever to publish a paper!
